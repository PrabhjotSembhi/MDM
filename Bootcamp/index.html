Frequently Asked Questions

You will be assigned a personal accountability partner who will guide you on a daily basis. This is not a pre-packaged “one-size-fits-all” diet plan. We believe in three meals per day and proper snacking habits. Our goal is to help you lose visceral fat without losing muscle mass, which is common with most diets.

It’s a five-day program divided into two phases. The first step is a 3-day “Fat Oxidation Jumpstart,” followed by 4 days of personal food plate monitoring and nutrition principles taught in 4 LIVE SESSIONS of 90 minutes each. The program does not involve meal replacements but real food. In these 5 days, we teach many of the principles that are a part of our 12 week structured program. And after the 5-day program is over, many people keep going on their own with the principles they have learned, and others opt for the 12-week structured program, which is a paid program, with daily monitoring, where 8-10 kg of fat loss is expected.
